Anokha Techfest 2023 the annual event of Amrita School of Engineering, Coimbatore provides an unique opportunity for students to participate in fun-filled and challenging events and workshops. The event throws open opportunities for students from all disciplines to challenge themselves and foster the “Can Do” attitude. One of the key highlights of the Anokha 2020 edition was the Green Anokha concept. It was so wonderful to see the students adopt and implement our Chancellor AMMA’s idea of “Living in Harmony with Nature”. It was plastic-free and eco-friendly Anokha – an initiative that will become a tradition!!”, Dr. Sasangan Ramanathan, Dean, Faculty of Engineering, Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham.

Awareness Initiative

Individually we all are drops, together we are an ocean. Anokha believes in involving thousands to bring a change in million realities. We organise various campaigns inviting the support of people around us to create an unforgettable impact on society. Many campaigns like sanitary napkin drive, notebook distribution drive, blood donation camp have been organised by Anokha.

Career Workshops

'Career workshop' was our initiative to leave our touch to their bright futures, making them build their dream career and live their desired life! It was an interactive session with the kids learning from them their dreams and enlightening them with various career opportunities.
